  • What are the main features?

    The Fender American Ultra Luxe Telecaster has an alder body, modern "D" maple neck, stainless steel frets, and Ultra Noiseless Vintage pickups. It features S-1 switching, a 6-saddle hardtail bridge, and a contoured neck heel for better access to higher frets.

  • Suitable for beginners?

    This is a high-end professional guitar, but serious beginners can enjoy its smooth playability, noiseless pickups, and comfortable neck profile. However, due to its premium price, it may not be the best first guitar for casual players. The modern “D” neck offers a great balance between comfort and playability.

  • Works for multiple genres?

    Yes! It’s great for rock, blues, jazz, country, and pop, thanks to its Ultra Noiseless pickups that provide vintage warmth without hum. It’s not ideal for high-gain metal, but adding a distortion pedal can help achieve heavier tones. The S-1 switch expands its versatility, making it suitable for various styles.

  • Right size for me?

    With a 25.5" scale length, 1.685" nut width, and a modern "D" neck, this guitar is comfortable for most players. The contoured neck heel allows easier access to higher frets, making it suitable for lead guitarists. The lightweight alder body ensures a balanced feel while playing.

  • Does it come with a warranty?

    Yes! Fender offers a limited lifetime warranty covering materials and craftsmanship defects. It does not cover accidental damage, modifications, or normal wear and tear. To activate the warranty, you should register your guitar on Fender’s website. This ensures support in case of any issues.

description

The American Ultra Series of guitars is Fender’s most advanced, and the American Ultra Luxe Telecaster fits right in. The Fender Luxe Telecaster features a unique augmented “D” neck profile with Ultra rolled fingerboard edges for hours of playing comfort and a tapered neck heel for easy access to the highest register. A speedy 10"-14" compound-radius fingerboard with 22 stainless steel medium-jumbo frets means effortless and accurate soloing, while the Ultra Noiseless Vintage pickups and advanced wiring options provide virtually endless tonal possibilities, without hum. Other features include sealed locking tuning machines, chrome hardware and bone nut. A hard shell case is included for portable protection and storage between gigs.
